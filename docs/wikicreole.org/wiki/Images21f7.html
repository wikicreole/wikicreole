Syntax for displaying in-line images and for linking them to another page.  Also see [[images reasoning]] for creole's decisions.

{{{
{{myimage.jpg}}
{{myimage.jpg|this is the alt text for my image}}
[[some link|{{myimage.jpg}}]] - if you click on the image, will goto "some link"
[[http://example.com/|{{myimage.jpg}}]] - same as above: picture links to url
}}}

Before we were calling this [inclusion] or [transclusion|http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transclusion] which would do more than just images, but that is not required for a conforming Creole implementation.
