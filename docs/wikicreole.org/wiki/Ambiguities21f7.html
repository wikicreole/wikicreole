Here we collect known markup collisions and strategies to handle them when implementing. The ambiguity pages also serve us to document test cases on how to detect problems in engine implementations with these ambiguities. Developers can report about their strategies here on how they solved the issues. This way others developing in different languages and engines/tools can learn from them.
