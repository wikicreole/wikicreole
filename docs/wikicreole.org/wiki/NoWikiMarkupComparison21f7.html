* //nowiki// - no markup is interpreted between the tags
* //monospace// - text is set with monospace (teletype) font, spaces may be preserved
* //monospace+nowiki// - like {{{ {{{...~}}} }}} in Creole
* //pre// - monspace paragraph with spaces and newlines preserved, no wiki markup
* //nolink// - way to disable WikiWords
