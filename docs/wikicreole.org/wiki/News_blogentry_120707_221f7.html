!! Creole 1.0 is live!

Last week we sent out a [[WikiCreolePressRelease|press release]] to announce that Creole 1.0 is stable and live.  In the past week, developers of the two most popular wiki engines ([[TWiki]] and [[MediaWiki]]) have been discussing how to best implement it.  We will be having another IRC meeting on July 24 at 21:00 GMT to discuss ways to [[promotion|promote Creole]].
