[[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drupal|Drupal]] is an open source CMS (Content Management System).

It supports Creole through the [[http://drupal.org/project/pearwiki_filter|PEAR Wiki Filter]] module. The [[http://pear.php.net/|PEAR]] [[http://pear.php.net/package/Text_Wiki|Text_Wiki]] and [[http://pear.php.net/package/Text_Wiki_Creole|Text_Wiki_Creole]] packages are not included, but they are also required. It is suggested you get the latest version through cvs.

See the [[http://drupal.org/project/pearwiki_filter|module page]] and the [[http://cvs.drupal.org/viewcvs/drupal/contributions/modules/pearwiki_filter/readme.txt?view=markup|readme file]] for installation and configuration details.
